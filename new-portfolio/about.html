about me

welcome

Hi! I'm Gary and I'm brand new to coding and web development.

I spent a good few years after graduating from University in pretty menial jobs, saving up for a few big trips travelling, volunteering and working abroad.

I got back to the UK in 2017 and became an adult, I now have a ring on my finger, two kids at my feet and a mortgage on my back. For the last 4 years I've been working for a Complex Needs Housing charity and as a Family Support Worker for a Special Needs School. Both have been amazing and invaluable to my personal development but after continuously banging my head against the brick wall of a system which really didn't care, I decided I'd make a drastic career change rather than risk my sanity. That leads me here!

Two close friends have recently made a career change and the switch to coding, both completing the Northcoders course I am about to embark upon. I started dabbling with a few of the basics and enjoyed the structure, logic and focus on problem solving more than I expected!

I had hoped to start the course in early 2020 but the combination of a 17 month old, a newborn and lockdown meant that realistically I couldn't fully apply myself to the course and fulfill my parental duties!

Now, in early 2021 I'm starting Northcoders and can't wait to expand my development horizons!
